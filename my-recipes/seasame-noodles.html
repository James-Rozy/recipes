Seasame Noodles

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of thin spaghetti or angel hair
  • 2 Tbsp seasame oil
  • 4 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 Tbsp rice vinegar
  • 2 Tbsp tahini
  • 2 Tbsp toasted white seasame seeds, mortar and pestled
  • 1 tsp black seasame seeds
  • 4 green onions, finely sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, micro planed

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta in boiling water and reserve a cup of the pasta water.
  2. Combine the seasame oil, soy sauce, rice vinegar, garlic, and ground white sesame seeds in a seperate bowl
  3. Stir the sauce into the cooked pasta using the starchy pasta water to develop the perfect sauce consitency
  4. Add the green onions and black seasame seeds to finish it off!
  5. Place in a bowl and start slurping!
